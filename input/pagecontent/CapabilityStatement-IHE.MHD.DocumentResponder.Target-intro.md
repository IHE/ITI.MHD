The Document Responder may declare the [XDS on FHIR](1332_actor_options.html#13322-xds-on-fhir-option) Option or the UnContained References [Options](1332_actor_options.html). This Actor SHALL provide a server implementing [Find Document List ITI-66](ITI-66.html), [Find Document References ITI-67](ITI-67.html), and [Retrieve Document ITI-68](ITI-68.html) transactions.
